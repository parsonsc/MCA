Services

Acquisition

Source and advise on current opportunities on either an open market or off market basis.
Our investment team represent buyers at all stages of the process, from advice on selection and location to opportunity sourcing and due diligence. We strive to deliver the best possible return for our clients. We adopt a thorough and diligent approach to property transactions and pride ourselves on our ability to identify opportunities and see them through to completion.

Management

Once an investment has been acquired, M-CA are able to assist in every aspect of the day to day running of the property to ensure maximum performance is achieved. Each service is tailored to the individual clients needs and allows for a worry free investment.


Management Services Include:
  • Letting Advice
  • Rent Collection
  • Day to Day Management
  • Maintenance
  • Furniture Supply

Analysis & Advisory

Further to the management services, M-CA are able to create reports and bespoke analysis of the investments performance during the hold period. Where appropriate, M-CA provide additional advise on their acquisition to further enhance the performance of their investment.

Analysis & Advisory Services include:
  • Quarterly asset performance analysis
  • Market update and monitoring
  • NRLS Tax Advisory
  • Currency FX Advisory

Development Management

M-CA has extensive experience in design-led development projects of every scale, with the flexibility to enter the venture at any stage; a service that complements our acquisition department. We carry out development appraisals to assess potential value and returns for existing holdings and potential acquisitions. M-CA are able to manage the planning process and work with our partner architects and consultants to produce the most prosperous design.

Each service is bespoke to our clients needs whether it is a £500,000 single acquisition or a £25m portfolio M-CA will ensure the same level of service is provided and all our clients objectives are achieved.
